# FOH & BOH Staff Onboarding SOP Templates
## Australian Hospitality Compliance Toolkit — Asset 2 of 2

---

> **What this template is:** A practical onboarding tool for Australian hospitality venues. It is not a substitute for qualified HR, legal, or regulatory advice. Employment obligations are governed by the Hospitality Industry (General) Award 2020 (MA000009) and applicable state and federal legislation. Confirm current requirements with Fair Work Australia and your state food and liquor regulator.

> **How to use it:** This template is structured around a 30-minute manager commitment per new hire — not the total training time, but your active admin time across four weeks. Three fast windows: 10 minutes for Pre-Start Check (Section 2), 10 minutes for Day 1 Orientation Check (Section 3), 10 minutes for Week 4 Review (Section 6). Sections 4 and 5 are delivered by a senior trainer or completed by the staff member directly. Duplicate this page for every new hire. Store completed copies in your venue's HR folder alongside the employment contract and RSA certificate.
