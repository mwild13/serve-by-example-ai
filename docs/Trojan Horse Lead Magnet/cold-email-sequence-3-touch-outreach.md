## Personalisation guide

The opening observation is the only manual element. It takes 10–20 seconds per contact. The options in order of specificity:

1. **Recent post or review** — "Saw [Venue Name] got a mention in [publication / group post]."
2. **Multi-state signal** — "Noticed you're across NSW and QLD."
3. **Venue size/type signal** — "Saw you're running a [pub / restaurant group / late-night venue]."
4. **Directory lookup** — "Found [Venue Name] through [AHLA / hospitality directory]."
5. **LinkedIn signal** — "Saw you're heading up operations at [Group Name]."

Never fabricate specifics. If nothing specific is available, use option 4 or 5.

## Sending notes

- Send plain text only. No HTML, no images, no logos.
- Send from a real inbox (Mitch's name, real reply-to address). Not a no-reply.
- Max 30–40 new contacts per day per sending domain to protect deliverability.
